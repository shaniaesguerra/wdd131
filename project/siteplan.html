Website Planning Document

Site Name:

Eliah's Bakery

  • This name of the bakery is a combination of my brother's name and my nickname. The name represents the brand and our identity as Filipino immigrants. We like to give our local community a taste of authentic homemade Filipino baked goods and give them our own twist of classic Western baked goods.

Site Purpose:

The site provides information about the brand, the business (how it operates), information about the products and their prices, contact information, and a form for placing an order and a review;

Scenarios:

Questions asked by the target audience:

  • What are the ingredients of the products?
  • Where and how do I order?
  • Where is the organization located?
